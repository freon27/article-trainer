Thank you for using Article Trainer!

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect any information you provide when using our mobile application.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users of our app. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected.

2. Error Logging

We use Sentry, a third-party error logging service, to collect information about app crashes and errors.

The data collected by Sentry is used solely for the purpose of identifying and fixing bugs to improve the app's performance.

3. Cookies

We do not use cookies or any similar technologies that track your usage of the app.

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated Privacy Policy in the app. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
